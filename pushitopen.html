The gap from the door lets in the alluring scents go the kitchen and the laughs of the two head servers. The Maid shakes free of her reverie.“Nice to meet you sir,” She says bobbing the obligatory curtsy and hurries past him to the last of the evening’s sunshine in the kitchen garden. She finds Sam the kitchen boy who helps her avoid a scolding from the cook. Sam who has kind eyes, an open manner and someone who maybe good for her. Someday.
