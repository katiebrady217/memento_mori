EXIT

As we’ve said the girl’s Grandfather has always had a mind full of sound logic. He knew his social and political standpoint had taken a significant downturn thanks to his foolish son’s part in bringing this unwanted child into the world. He had to think “What If?”.

He blinks. In the space of that single second the little girl is no longer on those steps. She lies on the cold, cold marble floor. Still. Everything is still.

THE END
